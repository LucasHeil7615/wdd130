BB Droid

There once was Ball turned droid.

Who often contemplated the void.

BB was in the circular motion of space.

Why was the Ball in this place?

Then the droid was in a spaceship soaring,

He spent 100 light-years exploring

He was always excited by the engines roaring

Each trip brought circular spheres of color,

And species and all sorts of culture

With each discovery, his life before became duller.

Yet, often times when gazing out into the stars,

all he could think of were the scars

Time had its tole on his friends this past century

Yet still, all these will be held in his memory.
